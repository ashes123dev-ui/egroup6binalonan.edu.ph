Information Technology

Information Technology focuses on the study and application of computer systems, software development, data management, and network administration. Students learn how to design, develop, and maintain digital systems that support modern businesses and organizations. The course also covers programming, cybersecurity, web development, and database management, preparing students for careers in the fast-growing technology industry where problem-solving and innovation are essential.

Business Administration

Business Administration focuses on the principles and practices of managing organizations effectively. Students gain knowledge in marketing, finance, human resources, operations management, and entrepreneurship. The course develops leadership, decision-making, and strategic planning skills needed in the business world. It prepares graduates for careers in corporate management, business ownership, banking, and various administrative and supervisory roles in different industries.

Education

Education prepares future teachers with the knowledge and skills needed to effectively teach and guide learners in different educational levels. The course covers child development, teaching strategies, curriculum design, classroom management, and educational psychology. Students are trained to become effective communicators and mentors who can inspire and support students' academic and personal growth in a structured learning environment.

Hospitality Management

Hospitality Management prepares students for careers in the tourism and service industry, including hotels, resorts, restaurants, and event planning. The course emphasizes customer service, business operations, food and beverage management, and hospitality marketing. Students are trained to handle real-world hospitality situations with professionalism, efficiency, and strong interpersonal skills, ensuring guests receive high-quality service experiences in both local and international settings.

Criminology

Criminology is the scientific study of crime, criminal behavior, law enforcement, and the justice system. This course equips students with knowledge in forensic science, criminal investigation, sociology, and psychology related to crime. It also develops skills in analyzing criminal patterns and understanding the causes of criminal activity. Graduates are prepared for careers in law enforcement agencies, security services, forensic departments, and public safety organizations.

Midwifery

Midwifery is a healthcare course dedicated to maternal and newborn care during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postnatal period. Students are trained in safe delivery practices, prenatal care, newborn assessment, and emergency response during childbirth. The program emphasizes compassion, professionalism, and medical knowledge to ensure the safety and well-being of both mother and child. Graduates typically work in hospitals, clinics, and community health centers.

Accounting

Accounting focuses on the recording, analysis, and reporting of financial transactions for individuals and organizations. Students learn about bookkeeping, auditing, taxation, financial reporting, and cost management. The course develops strong analytical and mathematical skills needed to interpret financial data accurately. Graduates are prepared for careers in accounting firms, corporate finance departments, government agencies, and auditing institutions.

History

The University of Eastern Pangasinan (UEP) Binalonan Campus was established with the vision of bringing accessible, affordable, and quality education to students in Binalonan and nearby towns in Pangasinan. It was created to support the growing demand for higher education in the region and to help develop skilled professionals who can contribute to local and national development. Since its establishment, UEP Binalonan has continued to expand its academic programs, facilities, and student services. The institution focuses on providing practical and industry-relevant education in fields such as Information Technology, Business Administration, Education, Criminology, Hospitality Management, Midwifery, and Accounting. Through the years, the university has remained committed to its mission of shaping competent, responsible, and service-oriented graduates. It continues to strengthen its role as a learning institution dedicated to academic excellence, character formation, and community development in Pangasinan.

Administration

The administration of UEP Binalonan is composed of dedicated academic and administrative leaders who oversee the overall operations of the university. They are responsible for maintaining academic quality, managing school programs, and ensuring a supportive learning environment for students and faculty.

UEP earns Reporter Status at THE Asia University Rankings 2026

University of Eastern Pangasinan (UEP) obtained a “Reporter Status” at the Times Higher Education (THE) Asia University Rankings 2026. This status reflects the university's continuous commitment to internationalization by participating in global university benchmarking activities.

Reporter status means that UEP has submitted institutional data to THE and is now part of its global benchmarking process; however, it is not yet included in the ranked list until it meets the eligibility criteria.

This achievement serves as a foundation for UEP's future inclusion in international rankings, reflecting its pursuit of global excellence through quality education and service.
